Help

Here are some tips and commands that will help you have the best experience with Kirino:

Chatting

Just type whatever you like, like if you were chatting with a friend!.

Text to Speech

Kirino can talk!, you can turn it off or on in the settings tab. This configuration is saved locally, so you can have certain devices where you want her to talk. The voice depends on your OS and Browser.

Night Mode

You are on your cellphone or tablet in the night? Night Mode is for you, it will reduce the brightness. Type: "Night mode"

Change settings

In the settings tab you'll be able to change your name, city, your assistant's name and more.

Date and Time

Type: "Tell me the date" or "What date is it"

Mailing

Type: "Send mail to email@sample.com"

Facebook

Type: "Post to Facebook" or " Facebook Post"

Twitter

Type: "Tweet" or "Post to Twitter"

Google+

Type: "Post to Google" or " Google Post"

Arithmetic

Type: "Solve OPERATION" Ex: Solve 2+2

Graphing

Type: "Graph EQUATION" Ex: Graph 3x-1

Open websites

Type: "Open WEBSITE" Ex: "Open Facebook"

Web Search

Type: "Pictures of WORD" Ex: "Pictures of koalas"

Images Search

Type: "Search WORD" Ex: "Search Hyuchia Corporation"

Weather in your city

To get the current weather in your city Type: "Weather" or "Climate" (Your city must be set in the settings tab)

Weather in somewhere else

To get the current weather Type: "Weather in CITY" or "Climate in CITY"

Google maps

Searching some location? Type: "Maps PLACE"
